The Code for NL Foundation is the Dutch network of open source developers and designers who collaborate on the digital government.

Our goal is the successful digital transformation of municipalities, other governments and society as a whole.

To achieve this, we involve as many designers and developers, to shape the new digital society together to give.

We can use some help with that!

For example, we are looking for someone to help us with it organizing the monthly developer meetups.

And someone who helps with our ideas and important ones publish developments regularly on our blog, at twitter, our website and in presentations.

Would you like this? Then we would like to meet you! Send a mail to info@codefor.nl with your name, contact details and short motivation. We take then contact you as soon as possible!

Stuur een mail!

Code for NL is a governing partner of the Code for All network, an international network of civic innovators who are committed to open, transparent and democratic digital society.
